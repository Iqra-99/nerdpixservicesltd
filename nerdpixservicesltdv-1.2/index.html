FAQ

What services does Nerdpix Services offer?

We provide a wide range of IT and security solutions including custom software development, web and mobile apps, cloud computing, IT consulting, cybersecurity, CCTV installation and maintenance, access control systems, turnstile integration, and security guarding support.

Who are your typical clients?

Our clients include businesses of all sizes needing IT infrastructure, software solutions, or integrated security and facilities management support. We work closely with security firms, property managers, and companies looking to enhance both their digital and physical security.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer continuous technical support and maintenance for both IT systems and security installations to ensure your operations run smoothly and securely.

Can you customize solutions for my business needs?

Absolutely! We tailor our software, IT services, and security systems to match the unique requirements of your business, ensuring maximum efficiency and protection.

How do you ensure the quality and reliability of your security services?

Our team combines technical expertise with on-the-ground experience. We follow industry best practices in installing and maintaining CCTV, access control, and security systems, and provide trained personnel to support your security needs.
